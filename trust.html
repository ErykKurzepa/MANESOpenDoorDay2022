Project T.R.U.S.T.
Marking of controlled components

MAN Energy Solutions has identified a number of vital components/processes called Controlled Components, which can only be manufactured by a vendor/licensee that is approved through a First-Time production Approval (FTA) procedure.

Key words in T.R.U.S.T.

  • Transparency: Controlled components only to be produced by approved vendors
  • Reliability: Reduced risk of component failures – leading to higher reliability
  • Unity: Approved Vendors and MAN ES has common interest
  • Safety: Increased marine and environmental safety
  • Traceability: Verification of origin of components
